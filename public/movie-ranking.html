Tasharna's Favourite Movies

Here are 3 movies to consider watching this year.

The Book of Clarence

This movie takes on a different, yest also comedic approach to the story of Clarence and Jesus Christ. Produced by Jay Z himself, this movie includes some amazing black actors and actresses from around the world.

Barbie

This one here is a strong 10/10. Eventhough this movie is based around the infamous doll from our childhood, it also picks up on issues young women could be struggling with in today's society.

Talk to Me

Now if you're into horror movies and love a good jumpscare, then this is something I would highly consider watching.
